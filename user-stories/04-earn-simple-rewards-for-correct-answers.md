## User Story 4 - Earn simple rewards for correct answers

As a child,  
I want to earn small rewards when I answer correctly,  
so that practicing feels fun and motivating.

### Acceptance Criteria

- Given I answer a question correctly,
  when feedback is shown,
  then I earn a visible reward for that answer.

- Given I complete a session,
  when the summary is shown,
  then I can see how many rewards I earned during that session.

- Given I complete a session,
  when the summary is shown,
  then I see a small celebration or positive success moment.
