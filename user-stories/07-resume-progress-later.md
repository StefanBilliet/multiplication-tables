## User Story 7 - Resume progress later

As a child,  
I want my unlocked tables and progress to still be there later,  
so that I can continue where I left off.

### Acceptance Criteria

- Given I have unlocked one or more tables,
  when I reopen the app later,
  then those tables are still unlocked.

- Given I have existing progress,
  when the app loads,
  then the app shows my current progression state.
